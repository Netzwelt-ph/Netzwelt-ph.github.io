Frequently Asked Questions


Can I submit a leave request?

Only bonafide members of the team are allowed to submit an approved leave request. The operative word here is "approved" meaning you need to secure permission from your team first.

What if I forgot to submit an approved leave request?

It will be treated as "Absence without leave" or AWOL and will be dealt with an appropriate disciplinary action.
